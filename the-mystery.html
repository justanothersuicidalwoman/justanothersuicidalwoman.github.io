Mystery of the Excluded Middle

The mystery of the Excluded Middle is an experimental essay exploring the law of the excluded middle, alongside the laws of identity and non-contradiction—the triadic foundation of Western logic. It then looks for the gaps in these rigid laws in performance art and theatre.

This essay was presented in an experimental format during 17th Conference of the Society for Artistic Research (SAR) in University of Galway in 2026.
